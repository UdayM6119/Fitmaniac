❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Reach out on social if you need more help.

What is Fitmaniac?
+
Fitmaniac is a comprehensive gym management and member experience platform for India. It connects gym owners (via the Gym Owner Portal), super administrators (via the Admin Console), and athletes (via the Customer Mobile App) in one seamlessly synchronized ecosystem powered by real-time data sync, smart QR check-ins, and gamified fitness challenges.
How do I join a Fitmaniac gym?
+
Visit a Fitmaniac partner gym near you and ask them to register you on the platform. Your gym owner will create your member profile, assign a membership plan (Monthly/Quarterly/Yearly), and issue your unique digital QR pass. Once set up, you can log in to the Fitmaniac Customer App to access all features instantly.
Is the QR check-in system secure?
+
Yes. Fitmaniac uses Dynamic Security QR Codes that rotate with a new unique token every 30 seconds. Each code is cryptographically tied to your member ID and gym token (e.g., FITFREAK_001), making pass-sharing physically impossible. The gate terminal plays an audio chime on access granted and a buzzer on denial.
What are carry-forward absent days?
+
Carry-forward absent days are compensation days added to your subscription for days you couldn't attend the gym due to illness, travel, or personal reasons. This feature is exclusively available to Yearly plan subscribers and is capped at a maximum of 30 days per year. Monthly and Quarterly members are not eligible for carry-forward days.
How do flexible 2-hour workout slots work?
+
Instead of fixed session times, Fitmaniac lets you book a preferred 2-hour window for your daily workouts (e.g., 6AM–8AM, 8AM–10AM, etc.). Each slot displays a live occupancy meter showing current crowd density — green for optimal, yellow for moderate, and red for peak. This helps you plan your visit to avoid crowded periods and get the most out of your workout.
How can I renew my gym membership?
+
Membership renewal is simple. When your pass is nearing expiry, a glowing amber banner appears in your app. Tap "⚡ PAY INSTANT UPI" to launch the checkout modal, which generates a dynamic transaction reference ID and a BHIM UPI QR code for instant payment. Alternatively, you can pay cash at the gym desk and your owner will mark it as an offline payment in the system.
What are Segment Challenges?
+
Segment Challenges are timed fitness circuits (inspired by Strava segments) that your gym owner publishes for all members. Athletes complete the circuit, log their time using the in-app stopwatch modal, and compete on a real-time leaderboard. The system validates personal bests automatically and updates your position among co-members. Example: "HRX 15-Min Strength & Conditioning Circuit."
Does Fitmaniac work without an internet connection?
+
Yes. Fitmaniac features a robust LocalStorage-backed Offline Sync Engine. All critical data (member rosters, classes, challenges) is cached locally. If the server goes offline, actions like registrations and renewals are queued in an offline buffer. When connectivity returns, a background sync worker automatically replays the queued operations to the server and reconciles all data — ensuring zero data loss for gym owners.
How do gym owners partner with Fitmaniac?
+
Gym owners can apply to become a Fitmaniac partner by reaching out to us via the contact information below or through our social media channels. Once approved, a Super Admin will register your gym on the platform, assign your unique barcode token (e.g., FITFREAK_001), configure payment methods, and onboard you to the Gym Owner Portal. The process typically takes 24–48 hours.
What is the Streak & Badge System?
+
Fitmaniac tracks your consecutive workout days to build a visible streak counter. Reaching milestone days earns you digital achievement badges: 🥉 Bronze Badge at 5 days, 🥈 Silver Badge at 10 days, and 🥇 Gold Legend Badge at 30 days. Earned badges are displayed on a visual badge shelf in your workout dashboard, making your dedication visible and rewarding your consistency.
